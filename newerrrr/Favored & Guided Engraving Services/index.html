Privacy Policy

1. Introduction
Welcome to Favored & Guided Engraving. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

2. Information We Collect
We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, place an order, or contact support. This includes your Name, Email Address, Phone Number, and Order/Personalization details.

3. How We Use Your Information
Your personal data is strictly used for:
• Processing and fulfilling your custom engraving orders.
• Communicating with you regarding your order status.
• Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
• Securing your account details.

4. Data Protection & Sharing
We implement standard security measures to protect your data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information.

5. Your Rights
Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to be informed, access, rectify, or request deletion of your personal data. If you wish to update your data or delete your account entirely, please contact us through the chat widget.
